United We Lead:
 Empowering Future Leaders

The National Patriot Scholars Congress (NPSC) 2024 brought together DOST scholars from across the country to foster collaboration and leadership in nation-building. Held at The Manor, Camp John Hay, Baguio City, from September 3 to 6, this prestigious event served as a platform for over 500 scholars to deepen their commitment to serving the Filipino people.

Representatives from ADS-HAUSC, Jan Michael Policarpio and Camille Janh Nolasco, proudly took part in discussions, networking opportunities, and interactive sessions designed to shape the next generation of leaders. Key figures, including DOST Secretary Renato U. Solidum Jr. and former DOST-SEI Secretary Dr. Josette Biyo, provided invaluable insights on leadership and public service.

With the theme "United We Lead," the event emphasized the role of scholars in driving progress through scientific innovation and community development. Through this gathering, participants were inspired to become catalysts of change, reinforcing their sense of duty as Iskolar Para Sa Bayan. The scholars gained a renewed perspective on their responsibilities, equipping them with the mindset and skills necessary to contribute meaningfully to the nation.
