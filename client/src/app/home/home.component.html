Scholarship Opportunities

S&T Undergraduate Scholarships

The S&T Undergraduate Scholarships Program aims to stimulate and entice talented Filipino youths to pursue lifetime productive careers in science and technology and ensure a steady, adequate supply of qualified S&T human resources which can steer the country towards national progress.

Junior-Level Science Scholarships

The Junior Level Science Scholarships (JLSS) Program aims to: provide scholarships to support talented third-year college students pursuing science and technology degrees and ensure a steady supply of qualified S&T professionals to drive national progress.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join ADS-HAUSC and how can I become a member?

Only bona fide students of Holy Angel University (HAU) who are currently DOST scholars are eligible to join the Association of DOST Scholars - HAU Student Chapter (ADS-HAUSC). Eligible students can join by contacting us for the registration details.

What are the benefits of joining ADS-HAUSC?

As a member, you gain access to a supportive community of DOST scholars, networking opportunities, and academic resources. You can enhance your leadership and career skills through exclusive events, training, and outreach programs. Additionally, you stay updated on scholarship opportunities, internships, and initiatives that promote science, technology, and community engagement.

Does ADS-HAUSC provide financial assistance?

No. ADS-HAUSC is a student organization and DOES NOT provide financial assistance. The DOST-SEI is the official provider of the scholarship. However, we support scholars by fostering a strong academic community, offering mentorship, and sharing valuable resources about scholarship opportunities. Through our outreach programs and initiatives, we also strive to give back to the community, promoting education and helping future scholars access the support they need.
