The Association of DOST Scholars - Holy Angel University Student Chapter (ADS-HAUSC) is the official university-wide student organization for the DOST-SEI scholars in Holy Angel University which aims to create an environment that will nurture the students’ oneness while promoting the essence of science, technology, and environment.

Opportunities

S&T Undergraduate Scholarships

The S&T Undergraduate Scholarships Program aims to stimulate and entice talented Filipino youths to pursue lifetime productive careers in science and technology and ensure a steady, adequate supply of qualified S&T human resources which can steer the country towards national progress.

Junior-Level Science Scholarships

The Junior Level Science Scholarships (JLSS) Program aims to: provide scholarships to support talented third-year college students pursuing science and technology degrees and ensure a steady supply of qualified S&T professionals to drive national progress.

Who Can Apply?

Ongoing Grade 12 students in Negros Oriental who are expected to graduate at the end of SY 2024-2025.
STEM STRAND: All students belonging to the STEM class can apply.
NON-STEM STRAND: Must be in the top 5% of the class based on Grade 11 ranking.

Contact Us

Requirements

  • Application forms Forms C-J which can be downloaded at https://www.science-scholarships.ph/
  • Birth Certificate
  • Parent/Legal Guardian/Spouse's 2023 Income Tax Return / BIR Form 1701 / Employment Contract for OFW / BIR Certificate of Tax Exemption / Certificate of Indigency / Certificate of Employment with Compensation / Proof of Pension (Note: Only one of these documents, whichever is applicable to you)
  • Passport-sized ID with signature above printed name
  • Notarized Affidavit of Guardianship (For applicants not living with their parents)
  • Marriage Certificate (For married applicants)

Benefits

  • Living allowance of ₱6,000/month
  • Tuition and other school fees not more than ₱40,000/year
  • Book allowance of ₱10,000/year
  • Uniform allowance of ₱1,000.00
  • Transportation allowance
  • Thesis allowance of ₱10,000.00
  • Group health and accident insurance
  • Graduation Allowance of ₱1,000.00
